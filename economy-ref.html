Economy Reference

Upkeep — three separate tracks

Each upkeep pool is reduced by different effects; a reducer that names one track does not touch the others.

TrackHow it works
PursuitFixed by pursuit type: Monument 300, Power 200, Energy 0, all others 100. Luminous Court zeroes Civic-pursuit upkeep.
Armyretinue count x (retinue cost - Upkeep -X). Reduced by Levy Hall (-200/-300), Tannery/Armory/Saddlery/Butchery/Fletchery/Smokehouse (-200), ABF (-500), High Quartermaster (-2000).
InfrastructurePer-settlement upkeep in INFRASTRUCTURE. Trade Guild removes Primitive (innate) + Developed (mastery); College of Engineering removes Sophisticated.

Pursuit upkeep by type: Monument 300, Power 200, Energy 0, all others 100.
