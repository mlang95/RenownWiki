Cunning Actions

1. Intercept Caravan

Cost: 2000 gold

If passes, choose another player. The next turn that the target player is host, extort that player’s trade income.

Endorsed: Also extort 2000.

Note: intercept caravan only affects a single turn’s trade income. The target player still receives trade income on subsequent turns as normal.

2. Foster Rebellion

Cost: 2000 gold

If passes, choose another player. At the beginning of the next Bandit Mechanic Phase, place a bandit camp with 10 retinues in the target player’s Outlaw Country.

Endorsed: Also extort 2000.

Note: if the target player’s outlaw country has no room to spawn the bandit camp, increase their outlaw country by one territory and spawn the bandit camp there.

3. Raze

Cost: 2000 gold

If passes, choose another player’s settlement. Select one active pursuits, active infrastructure, or active build timer in that settlement — it becomes damaged. Its innate and mastery effects become inactive until repaired. If an active build timer was selected to be damaged, the build timer does not increment until it has been repaired.

Endorsed: Also extort 2000.

Note: wonders have immune razed and cannot be targeted by raze.

4. Destabilize

Cost: 2000 gold

If passes, choose another player. Extort that player’s tax income at the beginning of the next turn.

Endorsed: Also extort 2000.

Note: destabilize only affects the next single turn’s tax income from that player. The player still receives tax income on subsequent turns as normal.

Rule: Act of War

If a Cunning envoy is condemned while targeting a player with whom you hold a Non-Aggression Pact, that player may immediately perform a declare war action targeting the condemned player.

The Act of War triggers on the condemned result regardless of which Cunning action was performed.
