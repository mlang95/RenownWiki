Combat Keywords

Steady
Initiative cannot be reduced by Tactics.
Unwieldy
Initiative cannot be improved by Tactics.
2H
Cannot use a Shield.
Deadly
On a Focused Strike: increase AP by -5, and the defender may Parry or Recover only with a Focused roll.
Unstoppable
Parried only by a Focused roll.
Cleave
On a Focused Strike: roll one extra Strike die at your modified to-Strike.
Poison
When the Defender receives a Strike and rolls a Focused Save, it fails; the resulting wound may only be Recovered with a Focused Recover.
Nimble
Gain +1 Initiative in the first Skirmish of each Battle.
Drilled
Does not lose Endurance in the first Skirmish of each Battle.
Destroy Shield
On a Focused Strike: the target loses its Shield attributes for the rest of the Battle.
Blunder
At Initiative -2 or lower, your to-Strike is set to 6+, before other negative modifiers.
One Shot
May only be Equipped in the first Skirmish of a Battle. Requires a Tiltyard.
Parry
While not Fatigued, roll a d6 to attempt to Parry a Strike before the Save. On a 5+, the Strike is Parried.
Riposte
While not Fatigued, if you Focused a Parry against a Melee Weapon's Strike, you Riposte: your opponent immediately takes a Strike from your melee weapon. You can Riposte a Riposte.
Recover
While not Fatigued, if a to-Save roll fails, roll a d6: a result of X+ Recovers the retinue.
Serrated
-2 to Recover against this weapon's Strikes (worsens the defender's Recover roll by 2).
Tempered
A Focused Save succeeds, regardless of AP.
Fatigue Token
Each token is -1 to your Strike to a maximum of 6+; and Morale -1 (uncapped). If your modified Morale is ever 7+, your army Routs. Tokens stack.
Shielded
A stacking -1 penalty to the Strike roll (to a maximum of 6+). Sources: a shield's -1 to Strike.
Negate Tempered
Ignores Tempered: this weapon's AP can reduce the target's Save past 6+ (to auto-fail), defeating the Tempered floor.
Negate Riposte
The target's Parry can never Riposte this weapon's Strikes (a natural 6 Parry still cancels the Strike, but no counter-Strike follows).
