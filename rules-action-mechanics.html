Action Mechanics

Envoys

Envoys are the currency of actions. You must send an envoy to perform an action during the envoy Phase.

To send an envoy:

  • Costs 1 envoy.
  • You must have the appropriate cost to perform the action before Sending.
  • Declare: “I’d like to Send a [Domain] Envoy.”

Resolving an Envoy

  • The envoy begins with 1 or more influence, based on your Standing in the envoy’s domain, plus any applicable influence X modifiers.
  • Vote — each player, in clockwise order starting with the starting player, must vote: A. Support X — spend X influence to increase net influence B. Oppose X — spend X influence to decrease net influence C. AbstainNote: influence spending caps: minimum 1, +1 per standing in that domain. pursuits, infrastructure, & factions may further modify how much you can spend per vote.
  • Influence +/−X — pursuits, infrastructure, & factions may automatically apply influence X or trigger a specific effect.
  • Calculate net influence — add all values together.

Action Cost

Whenever you perform an action, pay the relevant cost (gold, doubt) and follow the pass Description. If endorsed, also perform the endorsed effect of that domain.
