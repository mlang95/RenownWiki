Seasons

The Realm cycles through four seasons. The current season's effect is applied during the Empire Phase, and the Rest Phase advances the season by one.

SeasonNameEffect
WinterFreezingAll Armies gain Speed -1; Sieges do not increment. Tax income collected.
SpringPlantingNo Host, Bandit Mechanics, Trade Income, Council Phase, or Diplomacy Actions. Gain +1 Envoy. Bandit Camps Spawn.
SummerCampaigningAll Armies gain Speed +2
FallHarvestHusbandry Mastery Effects are doubled.
