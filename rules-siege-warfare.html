Siege Warfare

  • If you lay siege during the envoy Phase, track the relevant siege timer(s).
  • When a siege timer reaches 0, resolve the siege before Rest Phase (negotiate → battle if needed → capture/sack).
  • For full Siege rules, see warfare → siege warfare.

Beginning a Siege

  • End a move army action to become adjacent to an another player’s settlement you’re at war with.
  • Your army must remain there for the siege timer and cannot take other actions.
  • If attacked while sieging — if you fallback or use defensive formation, the siege is broken and the siege timer is removed from play.
  • Settlements being besieged: cannot count pursuits for the purposes of Craft X; armies gain blocked except to sally forth; timers targeting the settlement do not increment; industry actions cannot target it.

Resolving a Siege

When a Siege Timer reaches 0:

  • If not yet negotiated, negotiate now.
  • If negotiate fails and there’s an army or garrison inside, battle in Battle Phase.
  • If the besieging player wins or there is no eligible army or garrison:
  • Capture: capture timer 1. When resolved, the settlement is under your control: the settlement receive your active infrastructure effects, gain it’s pursuits, tax income, and reach.
  • Sack: raze all pursuits; extort 1000 per settlement tier; reduce settlement by 1 tier. The player that controls the settlement removes all pursuits from one settlement ward of the sacking player’s choice, and removes the settlement ward from play. Set sack timer 2 — may not lay siege until resolved. Army gains blocked and strained until the sack timer is resolved.
