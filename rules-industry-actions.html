Industry Actions

1. Build

Cost: 2000 gold

If passes, choose an Unlocked, available infrastructure and set a build timer equal to that infrastructure’s build time. On completion: the infrastructure becomes active in all settlements in your province.

Endorsed: recoup 2000.

Note: you must have at least one infrastructure from the prior tier before you may build at the next tier. You may only have one active build timer at a time.

Note: wonders are built in your capital and require all other infrastructure to be active at the time the build action is performed.

2. Repair

Cost: 2000 gold

If passes, choose a damaged pursuit or infrastructure in a settlement you control. Set a repair timer 2. When the Repair Timer reaches 0, choose one:

  • Restore: Reactivate the pursuit or infrastructure with all innate and mastery Effects intact.
  • Demolish: Remove the pursuit or infrastructure from the settlement, freeing the settlement ward for a future pursuit.

Endorsed: recoup 2000.

3. Pursue

Cost: 2000 gold

If passes, choose an inactive settlement ward in a settlement you control. Select a pursuit whose prerequisites you currently meet, and set a build timer equal to that pursuit’s build time, usually build timer 1. On completion: place the pursuit in the chosen ward. Its innate effects activate immediately, and its mastery effects activate if all mastery requirements are met.

Endorsed: recoup 2000.

Note: you may have only 2 monument pursuits across your entire empire.

Note: power pursuits and unique pursuits have build timer +1. Monuments have build timer +2. All other pursuits have a build timer 1.

4. Charter

Cost: 2000 gold

If passes, choose one of the following:

  • Charter a New Settlement. Select an uncontrolled territory, or a territory in your Province that is not water or mountain, is at least range 4 from any other settlement, and not within range 2 of any Outlaw Country. Set a build timer equal to the village build time. On completion: place a new village on that territory.
  • Charter a Hamlet: If you do not have a Hamlet in your empire, select a territory in your province that is not water or mountain, and is range 2 from your capital. Set a build timer equal to the hamlet’s build time. On completion: place a new hamlet on that territory. Hamlets always allow you to pursue arable land, even if that raw material is not one in that region.
  • Expand an Existing Settlement. Select a settlement you control that is not a city, and a territory adjacent to it. Set a build timer equal to the next tier’s build time. On completion: upgrade the settlement by one tier (Village → Sea/Town, Sea/Town → Port/City, or Port/City → Metropolis), expanding into the chosen adjacent territory, placing a settlement ward.

Note: in order to resolve a build timer that would cause a town to become a city, or a city to become a metropolis, you must have the requirements needed to have that type of settlement. Otherwise, set the build timer 1.

Endorsed: Recoup 2000.
