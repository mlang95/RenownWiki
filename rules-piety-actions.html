Piety Actions

ActionCostRequiresEffect (if passes)Endorsed
Spread GospelDoubt 1—All non-allied players gain Doubt 1. All allied players gain Faith 1.Gain Faith 1.
Send MissionariesDoubt 1—Choose a target player or alliance. Outside your alliance → all players in the target alliance gain Doubt 2. Inside your alliance → all other allied players gain Faith 2.Gain Faith 1.
TitheDoubt 1—Choose a player. Extort 10% of their treasury (round down to nearest 100, min 0).Gain Faith 1.
ConvertDoubt 1—Choose another player's closest non-capital settlement; they must have Public Order −5 or lower. Lay Siege using only settlement-type siege modifiers (Wooden Walls, Stone Walls, Citadel, Garrison, Standing Army) and set a Convert Timer. At 0, the settlement joins your empire (see Capture). — If the target's Public Order rises to 1+ before the timer hits 0, the Convert fails and the timer is removed.Gain Faith 1.
CrusadeDoubt 1Sovereign PietyDeclare War on a non-ally with no NAP or truce, then immediately perform a Move action with one of your armies. — While a Crusade is active, neither player may Declare War, Sign/End Treaty, Negotiate, or Demand Tribute against each other.; A player may only be on one active Crusade at a time. It ends only when one involved player is Vassalized.Gain Faith 1.
