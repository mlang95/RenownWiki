Prowess Actions

1. Move

Cost: None

If passes, choose an army you control and move it up to its move range in territories. Then choose one of the following:

  • March: move the army up to twice its move range. The army gains −1 endurance. The army may not skirmish, lay siege, or muster this turn.
  • Skirmish: If at any point during a move action, the army is adjacent to a non-allied army not inside a settlement, end the move action and begin a battle with that army in the Battle Phase. Seize the Initiative: Gain +1I in the first skirmish.
  • Lay Siege: If the army is adjacent to a settlement owned by a player with whom you are at war during a move action, immediately end moving and begin a siege (see Siege Warfare).
  • Muster: If the army is within range 3 of one or more settlements you control, not within range 1 of a non-allied settlement or army, and you have an active muster field infrastructure, recruit retinues up to the combined muster limit of those settlements. You may also exchange retinues between adjacent allied armies and change equipment.

Endorsed: Also perform a move action (this second Move Action may target the same or a different Army).

Note: each army may only be the target of one move action per turn.

Note: strained armies do not gain +1 endurance at the start of the next turn.

Note: armies that have performed a skirmish, lay siege, or muster move may not perform another action until either the battle, siege timer, or muster timer has ended.

Note: Armies that have performed a skirmish gain +1I during the first Skirmish.

2. Declare War

Cost: None, requires Rising Prowess.

If passes, choose another player with whom you are not allied, do not have a Non-Aggression Pact, and do not have an active truce timer. Both players are now at war. Any trade agreements between you immediately end.

Endorsed: Also perform a move action.

Note: if your target is part of a defensive alliance, all members of that alliance are now at war with you.

Note: if you are part of a military alliance, all members of that alliance perform a declare war action on the target player next turn, and send 1 fewer envoys next turn.

3. Demand Tribute

Cost: None

If passes, choose another player with whom you are not allied and do not have a Non-Aggression Pact. Negotiate with the target player:

  • Target Agrees: sign a Non-Aggression Pact or alliance (Player’s choice from the offered terms).
  • Target Refuses or Fails to Fulfill Terms: Immediately perform a declare war action on the target player.

Endorsed: Also perform a move action.

Note: demand tribute may include any term that negotiate allows: gold, settlements, territory, treaties, or promises. Promises cannot be enforced, but unfulfilled promises trigger the refusal clause.

Siege Warfare

  • If you lay siege during the envoy Phase, track the relevant siege timer(s).
  • When a siege timer reaches 0, resolve the siege before Rest Phase (negotiate → battle if needed → capture/sack).
  • For full Siege rules, see warfare → siege warfare.
