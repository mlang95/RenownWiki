Turn Sequence

Each turn flows through five phases, then passes the Host and repeats. Click a phase for full detail, or any term for its definition.

1 · Empire Phase
Start of turn. Resolve start-of-turn effects, collect income, check Public Order.
Income Public Order
↓
2 · Council Phase
Players resolve Council business; Council Envoys are sent and voted before Domain Envoys.
Council Envoy Influence Abstain
↓
3 · Envoy Phase
Diplomacy Envoys first, then Domain Envoys. Send → Vote → Net Influence → Resolve.
Envoy Influence Endorsed Condemned Diplomacy
↓
4 · Battle Phase
Skirmishes, Sieges, and Battles resolve. Roll off for Initiative; fight in Skirmishes.
Skirmish Initiative Battle Siege
↓
5 · Rest Phase
Cleanup → Season +1 → score Renown → spend 1 Domain Point → pass the Host.
Season Renown Domain Point Edict
